Any Questions?

Do I need a domain name and web hosting with you to use the Website Builder?

Yes, you’ll need to purchase a web hosting package from us if you don’t already have one. You also need a domain name; we recommend registering or transferring your domain to us where possible for a seamless experience. Take a look at our Web Hosting and Domain Names pages for more details.

How do I access the Website Builder?

Once you’ve purchased a hosting package, log in to your control panel and go to ‘Web Builder’. From here you can access everything you need to build your website.

Can I back up my website?

You can download backups of your website whenever you like straight from your control panel.

Why do I need domain name?

A domain name serves as your unique online address, making it easier for visitors to find and remember your website. It adds professionalism to your online presence and helps establish your brand identity. Without a domain, your website would be identified by a lengthy and complex IP address, making it less user-friendly and memorable. In short, a domain name is essential for creating a credible and accessible online presence for your website or business.

Do I need to know how to code to use the Website Builder?

Not at all; everything is drag-and-drop or easily editable in a few clicks. If you’re comfortable with HTML, CSS, and/or JavaScript, you can edit and add code as you wish - but that’s completely optional.

What kind of support is included?

The Web Builder includes a comprehensive Help Centre with videos, tutorials, and on-screen guides. It’s intuitive and easy to use, but if you have a specific question or need further help, please contact our support team via your control panel.
